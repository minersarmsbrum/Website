## What We Built

You now have a professional website and a private staff portal — both connected and ready to go.

**Your website** (what customers see) lets people browse your menu, view the gallery, make a reservation, and get in touch. It's live on the internet, 24/7.

## Your Staff Portal

**To log in:** go to your website and add `/login` at the end.
> Example: `miners-arms.com/login`

There are two login levels:

| Login | What They Can Do |
|---|---|
| **Admin** | Everything — manage bookings, menu, gallery, and see stats |
| **Staff** | View only — see today's confirmed bookings |

**From the Admin dashboard you can:**
- See all bookings (past, present, upcoming) and filter by date
- Add, edit, or cancel any booking
- Update any menu price, dish name, or description
- Add or remove menu items and categories
- Upload or delete gallery photos
- Add a manual booking for phone-in reservations

## Automatic Notifications

You don't need to check anything. These happen on their own:

| What | When | How |
|---|---|---|
| **New booking alert** | The moment someone books online | Telegram message to your phone |
| **Daily morning summary** | Every day at 8am | Telegram message listing today's confirmed bookings |
| **Contact form alert** | When a customer fills in the contact form | Telegram message + email to you |
| **Guest confirmation email** | Immediately after a booking is made | Automatic email to the customer |
| **Cancellation email** | When a booking is cancelled | Automatic email to the customer |

You need the **Telegram** app installed on your phone to receive your alerts (it's free — iPhone and Android).

## How to Do Common Things

**Change a menu price**
Log in → Menu → find the dish → click the pencil icon → update the price → Save

**Add a new dish**
Log in → Menu → choose the section and category → Add Item → fill in name, price, description → Save

**Upload a photo**
Log in → Gallery → Upload Photo → choose file → add a short description → Save
*(The photo appears on the website immediately)*

**Delete a photo**
Log in → Gallery → find the photo → click the bin icon → confirm

**Add a phone booking**
Log in → Bookings → Add Booking → fill in the customer's details → Save

**Cancel a booking**
Log in → Bookings → find the booking → Cancel
*(The customer receives an automatic notification)*

## Your Social Media Bot

Three times a week — **Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 10am** — a bot messages you on Telegram and helps you create a post.

**How it works:**

1. The bot asks: *"Want to post today?"* — tap **Yes** or **No**
2. If Yes, it asks: *"Picture or Video?"*
3. For **Picture**: choose to upload a photo from your phone or let the AI generate one using your gallery images
4. For **Video**: send a food photo — the AI generates a short reel from it
5. The bot sends you a preview. Tap **Approve** (or ask it to try again)
6. Once approved, it posts to Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok automatically with an AI-written caption

The whole thing takes about 2–3 minutes.

**You have two bots in Telegram** — they appear as separate conversations:
- One sends your booking alerts and daily summary
- **@masocialsbot** runs the social media posts
